About The Dungeon House

The Dungeon House is a little place in the university district that holds unique events for friends and strangers alike. It is run by Madison Whiteman, Eli Ninninamann, and Nick Swagrin. These trio of dudes enjoy getting together with people and throwing some of the craziest games that redefines epic gaming. Along with their signature game, SwagMoney3’sdotcom, they throw other tournaments that include Nintendo events such as Mario kart, Mario golf, super smash bros, and more. The Dungeon house is a haven for those that love to play in the role-playing world of dungeons and dragons hosted by the dungeon master himself, Eli Ninninniaminn. Madison Whiteman has a vast collection of video games with multiple TV’s and controllers to hold gaming events like no other. Lastly, the house has a vast collection of board games that can include 2 to 12 people held in the house basement AKA the dungeon with a table that can seat 12 people.
If interested in the events held at The Dungeon house keep an eye out for our emails about upcoming events or navigate to the events page for more information.
